Audit and harden app-template workloads that are missing security contexts (`runAsNonRoot`, `seccompProfile: RuntimeDefault`, `allowPrivilegeEscalation: false`, `capabilities.drop: [ALL]`, `readOnlyRootFilesystem: true`).

Rollback/testing loop for each app or small batch:
1. Pick one low-risk app or coherent batch only.
2. Record current pod ownership/runtime assumptions: UID/GID, writable paths, PVC mounts, init containers, hostPath/device usage.
3. Apply the least disruptive security-context change first; avoid `readOnlyRootFilesystem` until writable paths are known or tmp mounts are added.
4. Validate manifests/schema locally.
5. Reconcile only that app in-cluster.
6. Wait for rollout and verify pods become Ready.
7. Check logs for permission errors (`permission denied`, read-only filesystem, failed mkdir/chown/open).
8. Exercise the app UI/API and any background jobs.
9. If any permission/runtime issue appears, immediately revert that app’s change, reconcile, verify recovery, and document the exception/path needed.
10. Commit only batches that have passed rollout + functional checks.

Prioritise internet-exposed and admin apps first; leave known privileged/host-level workloads documented as explicit exceptions.
